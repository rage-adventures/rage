What is Rage?

Rage Treks is an Eco Tour Agency focused on providing the most exhilarating and vivid adventures for those who crave for more adrenaline. Our Trek Leaders are experienced with captivating routes and excellent local knowledge of destinations which allow us to deliver a high standard of adventure. We have been delivering the finest trekking experience to our customers while preserving nature, culture & environment. Combining the outstanding customer service with priceless memories for all our customers.

Mission, Visson & Philosophy

In the last 2 years, the number of people engaging in activities like trekking, hiking & camping in Bengaluru has increased drastically., therefore Rage Treks wants to compete on a global level with a tourism product that is affordable and is also sophisticated, which also goes hand in hand with activities of sustainable tourism with social and environmental responsibility.

mission

Offer a personalized service of quality and reliability through careful attention to properly training our personnel, designing unique journeys at affordable prices, and exceeding the expectations of our clients. On the other hand, we have the mission to maintain principles of sustainability and responsible tourism in the destinations in which we operate, driving experiences to an elevated level of satisfaction for the travellers, which constantly propels us to achieve better efficiency in our services.

vision

Consolidate ourselves in the region as the leading tourism company in quality service, creativity, innovation, competitiveness, social responsibility promoting an ambience of good relationships, and obtaining the best client satisfaction.

Philosophy

Rage Treks is based in environmental protection and care, all made possible by highly qualified teamwork in the charge of our leaders who establish clear goals and expectations, in this way manifesting excursions for our adventurers which are ethical and conscientious.
