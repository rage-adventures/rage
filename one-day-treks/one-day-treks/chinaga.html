Chinaga Day Trek

Chinaga betta is situated very close to Tumkur town and one of the major attraction for the nature lovers. This trail starts from Torana anjaneya swamy temple and ends at Sri Lakshmi Ranganatha swamy temple. Enjoy the lush green forest around and fill your heart with joy.

Trek highlights

  • From here, we start ascending slowly and steadily until we reach a vantage point where you can see 360 degrees view of some really breathtaking views.

  • A quick breakfast and a 2.5 km trek will take you to the view point.

  • Away from the usual crowd. Get to experience the wilderness and taste fresh air without all those 'city dwellers'.

  • Attractions at every step. You never know which new sight to take a picture of.

  • Get rejuvenated with fresh air, clean water, and lush greenery.
