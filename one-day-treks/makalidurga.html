Makalidurga Sunrise Trek

If you are planning a break from the hustle and bustle of life, then Makalidurga is the place to be. The place is located in a quiet village at about 60 km from Bangalore and takes just short of two hours to reach the place. The highlight of the destination is the beautiful fort which lies atop a granite hillock. While the trekking trail here may not be as popular as that of Savandurga, it is nevertheless worth the experience. And the best part is, you won’t find it too taxing.

Trek highlights

  • Get transported to the foothills of history with this tour because nothing is better than getting lost in reveries that old things used to make back then.

  • Rejuvenate your mind amidst lush green trees and plants.

  • You can enjoy the scenic beauty range while you're at it.

  • You walk along the railway lines to reach the base of the trek, which is quite an experience.
