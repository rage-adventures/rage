Skandagiri Sunrise Trek

Skandagiri, also known as Kalavara Durga, is a mountain fortress located approximately 62 km from Bangalore city, and 3 km from Chikballapur in the Indian state of Karnataka. It is off Bellary Road (National Highway 7 Hyderabad-Bangalore Highway), and overlooks Nandi Hills and Muddenahalli. The peak is at an altitude of about 1450 meters. This famous hill is located in the Chikkaballapur district of Karnataka. Beautiful views from the summit with refreshments available. Overlooks Nandi Hills.

Trek highlights

  • Known for its scenic high-altitude beauty, Skandagiri is a perfect destination for those who prefer to unwind or just introspect.

  • You not only admire the serenity of the forest, but also get to witness the wonderful sunrise.

  • Witness the delightful sunrise above clouds at the top and enjoy a panoramic view of nature.

  • Experience the serenity of the mountain top and get a chance to see the ruins of Tipu Sultan’s fort.

  • Get rejuvenated with fresh air, clean water, and lush greenery.
